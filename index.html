Quick! The delivery is late! Sir Chef Prawn is too stressed to deal with this so it is up to you to choose a recipe from the folder and then fetch the right ingredients from the shelf before service starts!

  • Memorise all the ingredients on the recipe card
  • Browse the shelf and select all the right ingredients before the timer runs out!
  • Your score will be added to the leaderboard- see if you van reach the top!
